Dr. Jin Zheng

Dr. Jin Zheng has been a Lecturer in Data Science since 2021. Before that, she worked as a quantitative researcher in London for about three years. She leads a wide range of research in machine learning, deep learning, data science, natural language processing, and quantitative methods in finance, energy economics, and the Internet of Things (IoT). Additionally, she has an interest in sports prediction.
